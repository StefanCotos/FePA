About us
Why FePA?
 FePA is a website that helps both people and animals. Its purpose is to alert people about dangerous animals found in a specific area, but at the same time, it can also be a resource for injured or homeless animals that have the chance to be rescued or adopted by individuals with good intentions.
 Our objective is to make the world a friendlier place, both for people and for animals, so we've come up with a method to help both humans and animals.
Who are the founders?
 The two founders of this website are two students at the Faculty of Computer Science in Iasi Cotos Stefan and Burca Iulian-Stefan.They have a goal, that of making the world a better place.
